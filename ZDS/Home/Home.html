About Us

Zarra Studios was started by Marcus S. Zarra in the summer of 2005.  While Marcus is based out of Colorado Springs, Colorado, Zarra Studios is distributed with developers in several locations.

Marcus S. Zarra
Owner

Marcus is the owner of Zarra Studios and has been a developer for over 20 years.  He has been developing on the Macintosh platform since early 2003.  In the past he has been a developer at several Fortune 500 companies including Wells Fargo, Blue Cross Blue Shield and Verizon Business.
