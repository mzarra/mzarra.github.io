Welcome to Zarra Studios

Zarra Studios was founded in 2005 and has been developing Mac OS X software since its inception. With the release of the iPhone and the iPad, Zarra Studios has been providing high quality software for all three platforms. We have developed over 500 applications that are in the app store, many of which have been considered for Apple and other design awards.

Zarra Studios is dedicated to providing high quality software services for the Apple ecosystem. We provide both the graphical assets needed to produce an application and the engineering excellence needed to produce state of the art applications for both iOS and OS X.

With the wide breadth of experience our team brings to the table we are able to develop applications of any scale. Our team consists of designers, mobile developers, server developers and more.
